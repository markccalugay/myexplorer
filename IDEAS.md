# Ideas

Potential features to consider for future iterations.

- Walkie-talkie feature with push-to-talk support so users can speak to other devices in the trip or convoy in real time.
- Religious stop recommendations that can surface a pop-up for nearby churches, monasteries, and similar worship sites based on user preference, including Catholic churches and related destinations.
- Booking merchandise add-ons so users can order matching family t-shirts, hats, jackets, socks, towels, tiny flags, and stickers as optional extras when they book with us, plus the ability to order additional merch again after the trip.
